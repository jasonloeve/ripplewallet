  • How to restore funds from a paper wallet?

      Option 1 - Use the Ripply Android app


      Download the app: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ripplewallet.ripplewallet Scan the secret key QR to import it Now you are able to send your XRP


      Option 2 - Use one of the light Ripple clients


      This one has a nice interface: https://yxxyun.github.io/ripple-wallet/ This one is pretty raw & minimalistic: https://jatchili.github.io/minimalist-ripple-client/


      Option 3 - Use GateHub (requires identity verification)


      Register at https://gatehub.net Navigate to Wallets and click + (Add New) and import your Ripple secret key.
